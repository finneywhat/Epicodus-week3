Ping-Pong

To play, simply enter a number in the text-field below

Ping-Pong will count up to the number you've entered and display the results, with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 will be replaced with the word "ping".
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced with the word "pong".
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 will be replaced with the magical word "ping-pong".
