Ping-Pong

To play, simply enter a number in the text-field below.

Ping-Pong will count up to the number you've entered with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 will be replaced with "ping".
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced with "pong".
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 will be replaced with "ping-pong".
